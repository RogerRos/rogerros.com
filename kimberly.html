Did you ever like me?

Dear Kimberly,

If you pressed "yes," it means that despite my complete inability to act like a normal human, you somehow managed to see past the opaque facade that conceals a stupidly hopeful heart.

It took me hours to process that maybe, just maybe, you asked me to wait. But my glorious aspie brain interpreted that as a binary response, so I ran to my apartment, tripped, and destroyed my favorite pants. I wore them because I wanted to look my absolute best for you. I then proceeded to blast Black Sabbath at full volume while my neighbors debated whether to call emergency services.

What I am trying to say is:

There is a small place in my heart that will forever be free for you, and I am afraid my heart is not large enough to share with anyone else.

You have my email. I will be waiting for your message.
